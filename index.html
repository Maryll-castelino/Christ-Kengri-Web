CAPS

At its core, the Centre for Academic and Professional Support (CAPS) provides assistance in academic and professional spheres to anyone who may need it. The doors of CAPS are open to students, faculty, researchers, and anyone seeking support. At an individual level. It also provides a platform where one can develop networking skills and understand industry expectations through interaction-based university-wide sessions. It creates a forum for all the deaneries at an organizational level, thereby promoting the exchange of ideas and knowledge, leading to the optimal utilization of the university's vast resources.
